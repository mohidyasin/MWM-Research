Actively Managed Portfolio Solutions

Our Philosophy

AHF Research offers investment strategy solutions tailored for various environments and goals. Our aim is to outperform traditional approaches while minimizing both drawdown and risk exposure. Each investment system is actively managed and adjusted to an ever changing market environment and regimes. This contrasts with the conventional approach of buy, hold, and rebalance. We firmly believe individuals can obtain superior results by dynamically managing their investments instead of relying on traditional money managers or automated investment services.

Our Offerings

Equities

The flagship strategy is designed to provide superior risk-adjusted returns compared to traditional portfolios. It is a risk-on, equity-only portfolio that does not engage in short-selling and stays in cash when not invested in equities.

Crypto

Exposure to this alternative asset class with lower volatility and, consequently, minimized investment drawdowns. The approach stays limited to Level 1 crypto assets with strong fundamental and platform prospects.

Defensive

The Defensive strategy is most effective when paired with the Equities exposure. It aims to hedge against currency, inflation, and recession risks during risk-off periods.
